# hr-attrition-analysis-powerbi
HR Attrition Analysis (Power BI + SQL)
This project analyzes employee attrition patterns to identify key drivers of employee turnover across departments, job roles, income levels, experience, and overtime behavior.

Business Problem

HR leadership wants to understand:
Which employees are most likely to leave
Which departments have higher attrition risk
Whether overtime, experience, and income influence attrition
Which job roles require retention focus

Tools Used:
SQL (data exploration & attrition analysis)
Power BI (dashboard development)
Power Query (data cleaning)
DAX (metrics and KPIs)

Dataset:
IBM HR Analytics dataset (public dataset)

Key Metrics:
Total Employees
Attrition Count
Attrition Rate %

Overtime employees have significantly higher attrition risk
Low-income employees show higher attrition compared to other income bands
Sales department and Sales Representative role show elevated attrition risk
Larger teams amplify attrition impact
